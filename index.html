Food Fight

Description

Food Fight is a senior project developed by three students at Cal Poly. The team spent 20 weeks learning about game design and developing the game from scratch. My main contributions were building the collision detection and game state systems, and enemy artificial intelligence. You can read more about the project by grabbing a PDF copy of my report.
